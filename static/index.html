Welcome to Movie Guess Game

Rules

  • You need to Guess the movie by guessing one Alphabet at a time.
  • If you guessed a right alphabet then all the places with that alphabet will be releaved.
  • If you guessed a wrong alphabet you will lose a life.
  • You have total of 9 lives.

How to Play

You can choose an alphabet by:

  • Clicking the respective key on Keyboard
  • Clicking the buttons by mouse provided below.

Winning prize

What will you get if you win?

  • A virtual cookie.
